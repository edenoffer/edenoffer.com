About RPS

RPS is a design thinking tool and challenge generator for designers, that aims to push creative processes. it originated from the game “Rock paper scissors”, three random objects that make no sense together, however when put in a certain context they become completely sensible. the idea of creating a context for randomness to inspire creativity is the essence of this tool. it was created, in order to expended creative thought by trying to establish a different process. the tool has four sections, each one plays a rule in how we create & design. its not judgmental & its only intention is to push us outof the usual creation way. Www.RPSdesigntool.com the search for creativity is an endless and ever changing journey. i have invited talented individuals to join me in my search for a way, in to the randomness. we returned, full of new ideas & stories to tell, in the from of objects. all the projects presented here were done in less than 24 hours, using the rps tool over 12 months we did 30 projects useinf this menthed.
