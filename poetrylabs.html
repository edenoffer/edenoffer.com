About Poetry labs

Poetry labs ia a research group focused on developing wellness experiences. Using scientific neurological research to reduce anxiety and the feeling of depression through mobile experiences.

Read More

Poetry Labs vision

To develop a mobile platform with interactive experiences that reduce anxiety and provide on-demand methods to reduce the probability, and provide long term treatment for depression, accessible to anyone anytime

Clinical trials and experimental design

The Poetry Lab is a side project of mine that I developed during my research in the field of depression. This nonprofit project collaborates with clinics and academia in order to investigate new ways using techology to deal with depression.

I initiated this project from my own curiosity and past experience. I define Through research games and gamification methods, To tackle one of the most urgent problems current society is facing that is depression, Alongside side me there are five more individuals involved in the development of Poetry labs
